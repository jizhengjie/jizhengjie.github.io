Sindarin Lesson 1 - 人称代词与所有格

Collected from: Pedin Edhellen - a Sindarin Course

例文

Mae govannen!

Aragorn: Mae govannen!
Arwen: Mae govannen! Im Arwen.
Aragorn: Im Aragorn. Im adan. Ce elleth?
Arwen: Mae. Im elleth. Im sell Elrond.
Aragorn: E hır Imladris?
Arwen: Mae. E peredhel. Ce Rohir?
Aragorn: Law. Im Dúnadan.
Arwen: Cuio vae, Aragorn!
Aragorn: Cuio vae, Arwen!

 Well met!

Aragorn: Well met!
Arwen: Well met! I’m Arwen.
Aragorn: I’m Aragorn, a human. Are you an elven-maiden?
Arwen: Yes, I am an elven-maiden. I am Elrond’s daughter.
Aragorn: He is the lord of Rivendell?
Arwen: Yes. He is an half-elf. Are you from the Rohirrim?
Aragorn: No. I am a Dúnadan.
Arwen: Farewell, Aragorn!
Aragorn: Farewell, Arwen.

1.2 语法

所有格单数经常使用冠词”en”,对应英语中的”of”。它只能在所有者是普通名词的情况下使用。
例如：
cabed en aras (leap of the deer)

如果所有者是已经提到过的，或者是一个名字、不确定指代，则冠词”en”被省略。
例如：
aran Gondor (king of Gondor)
aras aran (a deer of a king)

在某些情况下（见 Lesson 12），冠词”en”被缩短为”e”。在复数形式中，所有格常常用以下方式来表达：
ion in erain (sons of the kings)

另一种比较少见的情况中，冠词”en”被写作”i”。这种写法和古时的复数形式”nia”十分相似。
例如：
cabed i aras (leap of the deer)
ion nia erain (sons of the kings)

如果所有格结构的第二个词是以辅音开头，那么辅音会发生移位，这个现象被称为突变。我们将在之后的 Lesson 3 和 Lesson 12 中讲到。

1.2.1 所有格

Presumably, there’s no explicit word for ’to be’ in Sindarin, at least as long as the context
is unambiguous. On the other hand, in all other verbs the personal pronoun is expressed
via the verbal ending (see lesson 2), so there is no need for nominative pronouns most of
the time. This leads to the idea that writing out personal pronouns explicitly is a way of
expressing ’to be’ in Sindarin:
Im Arwen. (I [am] Arwen.)
Ce aran. (You [are] King.)
Other pronouns can presumably be used in a similar fashion.
Sindarin has a courteous or formal form of 2nd person pronouns used to address high-
ranking personalities and possibly strangers.

Subject Singular Plural
1. Person im (I) mı́n (we)
2. Person(formal) *le (thou) *le (thou)
2. Person *ce (you) *ce (you)
3. Person(male) e, *so (he) *sy (they)
3. Person(female) e, *se (she) *si (they)
3. Person(things) *sa (it) *sai (they)
